# NCK BScN Study Assistant

AI-powered study app for Nursing Council of Kenya (NCK) BScN exam preparation.

## Features
- 147 MCQ questions across 14 nursing topics
- Practice, Timed (30s), and Exam modes
- RAG-powered AI that generates alternative questions from BScN textbooks
- Textbook evidence citations from BScN Paper I, II & IV
- Mobile responsive

## Topics Covered
1. Obstetrics & Midwifery (15q)
2. Adult Nursing (15q)
3. Fundamentals of Nursing (11q)
4. Community & Public Health (11q)
5. Pharmacology (10q)
6. Nursing Theory & Professionalism (10q)
7. Paediatric Nursing (10q)
8. Mental Health Nursing (10q)
9. Reproductive Health & Contraception (10q)
10. HIV & STIs (10q)
11. Anatomy & Physiology (10q)
12. Theatre Nursing (9q)
13. Evidence-Based Practice & Research (8q)
14. Critical Care Nursing (8q)

## Deploy
This is a static single-file app. Just deploy index.html to any static host.
